Pune Okayama Friendship Garden


Welcome to the Japanese Garden in Pune.
One of its kinds, the Pune Okayama Friendship Garden is the largest Japanese garden outside of Japan
This is the biggest garden of Japanese style out of Japan, in Asia.
The idea of recreating the original Japanese garden in Pune was, of course,
inspired from the 300-year old Okayama Korakuen Garden developed by Ikeda Royal family in the 12 specious hectares in Okayama.
