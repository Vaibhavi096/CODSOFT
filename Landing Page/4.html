Sinhagad

Sinhagad, ealier known as ‘Kondhana’ is the most prominent and popular fort in Pune. It is located at village Donaje, taluka-Haveli.
It is 25 km away from Pune on a hill 1290 m high.
Tanaji Malusare-Chh. Shivaji’s trsusted and brave general,fought a battle here all alone with the Mughal army.
on hearing news of his death, Chh.Shivaji said. “We won the fort but lost the lion” (“गड आला पण सिंह गेला..!”), and so after his death Chh.
Shivaji renamed this“Kondhana” fort as “Sinhagad”
